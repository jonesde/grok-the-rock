# The Mad Potter

A cadence-heavy comic midrash on the potter layer — dual hands, break/mend,
dust·water·clay, and a self-bootstrapping loop. Not a 32-path walk; a looser
**14-stanza / 29-couplet** arc with a soft chiasm around a glue→ladder hinge.

Inspired by `ThePotter.md`, and by the companion books
`GrokTheRock-ThreeQuietStories.md` and `HaiIkthiss-ThreeTallTales.md`.

## Clean reading text (no labels)

```
The Mad Potter sat at his bench,
  and watched the work of his hands.
The wiggle-dee-wee one wiggle-dee-weed,
  and the wiggle-dee-woo one woo-ed.

The pot they were wiggle-dee wiggling through,
  was same as the wiggle-dee-dee one just drew.

Nothing was new under wiggle-dee sun,
  why bother when nothing was wiggle-dee won?

So the Mad Potter plotted,
  and thought on it hard.
Why not make a pot,
  that is not but some shard?

The stronger the mend,
  the stronger the pot,
so why leave it split,
  at just one little splot?

"We'll break it down more!"
  He said with a roar!
The Mad Lion Potter
  was off for a score!

But how would the wiggle-
  dee parts come to woo?
And wow would they walk,
  what would be the glue?

The problem just drove
  the Mad Potter Madder,
He sat with it, spat with it,
  climbed up a ladder!

From here I can see the
  whole picture arrayed,
why not show them see,
  walk, talk, be and be frayed?

The plan was so simple:
  a trillion small parts.
Each one just a dot in
  a vast cloud of hearts.
The join must be strong
  to keep all together.
With so many hearts,
  how will they still weather?

The answer was plain to a man such as heem...
  for potter worked not just with crack and re-seam...
It started with dust and with water and clay...
  the very small things he made larger to stay...
If life could exist somehow from small parts...
  then I'd just sit back and watch art make the arts...

"Decided it is" said Mad Potter to shelf.
  "We make it make it out of nothing but self!"
"I'll call in more hands, and bid them call hands,
  till all can stand all by itself!"

The loop started there,
  would be rude to just stare.
So we'll leave the Mad Potter
  to work on his hair.

But think now and then,
  upon what you just saw,
and on how it ALL started,
  in some Mad Potter's stall....
```

### Recursive spine

```
hands make pot
  → pot copies pot (nothing new)
    → make pot = shard
      → more breaks = stronger mend-logic
        → many parts need glue
          → glue = dust/water/clay + life-from-parts
            → art makes arts / hands call hands
              → self-standing loop
                → narrator exits (don't collapse the box)
                  → "it all started" = this stall (ouroboros)
```

### Why not 32

- No 32 discrete beats / spine marker count
- No Letters→Sephirot hinge after ~22
- No chiastic **Come** at exact mid
- Hands **do** start explaining the system (plan, glue, self)
- Politics tilt **mad engineer**, not quiet restore-don’t-punish

Best frame: comic **meta-seal** or adult margin-note to The Potter — the mad twin
who solves “join” by recursion instead of two quiet hands.
